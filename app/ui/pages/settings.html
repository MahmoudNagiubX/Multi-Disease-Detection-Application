About this application

Multi Disease Detection System is an educational web application that combines machine-learning models for heart-disease risk estimation and deep-learning models for brain MRI classification (glioma, meningioma, pituitary, or no tumor).

The integrated AI doctor chatbot uses a large language model to help you understand your results and general medical topics in simple language. It automatically uses your latest heart and brain model outputs as context when answering.

Medical disclaimer: All predictions and explanations in this application are for educational purposes only. The models can be wrong, and nothing here should be treated as a diagnosis, prescription, or a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified doctor or healthcare provider for any decisions about your health.
